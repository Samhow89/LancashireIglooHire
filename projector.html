🎬 Projector Instructions

Follow these simple steps to use your projector

Power On

Use the remote control or the power button located on the projector to turn it on.

Connect Amazon Fire Stick

Plug the provided Amazon Fire Stick into the HDMI port on the projector. Connect it to your Wi-Fi via Settings > Network menu.

Streaming Services

Access Netflix, Amazon Prime, YouTube, and other streaming services using your own login details. Remember to log out after use.

Volume Control

Adjust the volume using the projector's remote controls or the buttons on the device itself.

Focus Adjustment

You can adjust the picture focus using the focus ring at the front of the projector for a crystal-clear image.

Using Your Laptop

To connect your laptop, use the provided HDMI cable. After connecting the power and HDMI cable, go to Settings > Display and select "Extend Display".
