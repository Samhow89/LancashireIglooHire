Projector instructions

Connect Amazon Fire Stick: Plug the provided Amazon Fire Stick into the HDMI port on the projector. Connect it to your Wi-Fi via the "Settings" > "Network" menu.

Streaming Services: You can access Netflix, Amazon Prime, and YouTube using your own login details. Please remember to log out after use.
